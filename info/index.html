make it real

It's easy to make this webpage into a real zine that you can hold in your hands. Just follow these simple steps:

  • Print the page.
  • Looking at it horizontally, fold the left and right sides to meet in the center. Then fold again in half.
  • Unfold and fold in half along the other dimension.
  • You should have 8 sections marked off by the folds.
  • Cut a slit in the middle of the paper at the top of pages 3/4 and 7/8.
  • Fold the page so that pages 1 and 2 are facing away from each other.
  • Push the paper together so that pages 3 and 4 are facing away from each other (and pages 7 and 8).
  • Fold it up so that the title page is on top. Now you've got a zine!
